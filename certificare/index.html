The system
Certificare is a web and mobile system (Android and iOS) for the commissioning process of different areas, focusing on the oil and gas field.

The system enables dynamic process modeling for a complete process structure for end-to-end monitoring of the commissioning process. The use of IoT to increase operational safety and Open Source interoperability. Certificare allows online monitoring of processes previously carried out on paper, improving communication between sectors and delivering information in real-time.
Users
Certificare has been used across the globe, with users in Korea, Singapore, and Brazil. 
The Design team had to establish connections and create UX Research strategies to get information and validation by these users with different cultures and scenarios.

My contributions

∙ Execute different  UX research methods, such as interviews, surveys, usability testing, desk research, heuristic analysis, and others.
