About

I'm a product Designer with a degree in Graphic Design and over six years of experience in web and mobile projects. I have worked for major national and international clients in various sectors, including technology, oil and gas, healthcare, and manufacturing.
I have experience in all stages of the design process, working in a user-centric way to improve their experience and ensure that the product meets their needs. I use different design tools such as Figma, Adobe XD, and other Adobe Creative Suite products, allowing me to create prototypes and designs that can be easily tested and implemented.
